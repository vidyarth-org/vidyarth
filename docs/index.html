Vidyarth

is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to supporting students with annual scholarships, school essentials, and healthcare support throughout their academic journey.

Our Initiative

Through a simple registration process involving both students and parents, we create a long-term support system that ensures continuity in education. Every year, registered students receive essential assistance designed to reduce financial burden on families and help students continue their academic journey with confidence.

We Provide

Uniform Fund ₹1000/year to help students purchase or maintain school uniforms.

Stationery Fund ₹1000/year assistance for books, notebooks, and essential learning materials.

Complimentary Health Checkup Basic annual health checkups to monitor student well-being and support healthy learning.

Answers to some questions

How can I find volunteer opportunities in my area?
You can use our website's search feature to browse local volunteer opportunities by entering your location and interests.
Are there age restrictions for volunteering through your platform?
Age requirements vary by organization and project, but we offer a wide range of opportunities suitable for all ages.
How do I sign up as a volunteer?
To get started begin browsing and applying for volunteer positions.
What if I have a specific skill to offer?
Many organizations welcome individuals with specialized skills. You can search for opportunities that match your expertise or reach out to organizations directly.
Do you provide training or resources for volunteers?
While training varies by opportunity, some organizations offer training programs. Additionally, we provide resources to help you prepare for your volunteer work.

Who We Are ?

Vidyarth is an India-based nonprofit organization working exclusively for students. We believe that no child should struggle to continue education due to lack of basic resources like uniforms, stationery, or healthcare.

Our Vision
A future where every student in India, continues education with dignity, stability, and access to basic resources and healthcare.

Our Mission
To provide continuous educational and healthcare support to students, ensuring their journey till 12th remains uninterrupted.
